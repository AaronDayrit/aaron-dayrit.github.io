About me

Here I am with Software Development. It was a no-brain option to me. I always had an artist's eye with the iron will of a thousand men. The perfect combo.

A lot of this fascination stems from my earlier experiences with technology, as you could say it was the canvas to the painting you are today.

More about me

I grew up in the blistering heat of Imus, Cavite, Philippines, for about half my life before I headed to the polar opposite of what we know now as Winnipeg. Family life was strict, especially with school. That's just what it was like in a south-east Asian country.

I always understood art better than my peers. To be able to able to translate the concepts of art into something understandable.

Just like the five senses, for me, artistry became one of them.
